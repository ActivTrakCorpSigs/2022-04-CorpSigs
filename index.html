Corporate Signatures

Gmail instructions

  1. Open Gmail on your web browser.
  2. Click the gear icon on the top right to enter Settings.
  3. Click See All Settings
  4. On the General tab, scroll down to the Signature section
  5. Click Create New then name your new Signature.
  6. Highlight all of the signature you would like to use from below then copy and paste the signature into the box in Gmail.
  7. Edit the FirstName, LastName, Title, Phone and email fields to reflect your information. Make sure to edit the links for the email and phone number as well.
  8. Under Signature defaults, select your activtrak.com email address and set it to use the signature you just created/named. Set the same signature for both New email and Replies/Forwards
